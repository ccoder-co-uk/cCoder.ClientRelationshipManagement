# Signed Contract Handoff Checklist

Date: 11 June 2026
Purpose: Define what must be complete before a won client moves from sales pipeline into the existing onboarding process.

## Handoff Trigger

Use this checklist when:

- Contract is signed.
- The opportunity is marked `11 Signed - Handoff Ready`.
- The delivery / onboarding team needs to receive the client cleanly.

## Required Before Handoff

### Legal And Commercial

- Signed contract stored in the client folder
- Legal entity confirmed
- Trading name confirmed
- Company number confirmed
- VAT number confirmed
- Registered office confirmed
- Commercial terms summary recorded
- Scope summary recorded
- Contract start date confirmed
- Billing / invoicing contact identified

### Relationship

- Primary commercial contact identified
- Primary operational contact identified
- Primary finance contact identified
- Primary technical contact identified, if relevant
- Stakeholder map recorded
- Relationship sensitivities recorded
- Original lead source recorded

### Delivery Context

- Agreed service or product type recorded
- Problem / pain summary recorded
- Promised outcomes recorded
- Important discovery notes recorded
- Known risks recorded
- Client expectations recorded
- Any deadlines or launch expectations recorded

### Materials

- Proposal stored
- Signed contract stored
- Relevant email history stored or summarised
- Any received client documents stored
- Any sent sales materials listed
- Notes from calls / meetings stored

## Handoff Document

Each signed client should eventually have:

`Clients/<Client Name>/Handoff.md`

Suggested sections:

1. Handoff Summary
2. Legal And Commercial Details
3. Contacts And Stakeholders
4. Agreed Scope
5. Promised Outcomes
6. Discovery Notes
7. Risks And Sensitivities
8. Materials
9. Onboarding Actions

## Handoff Acceptance

The onboarding owner should confirm:

- Required information is complete enough to begin.
- Missing information is explicitly listed.
- Sales remains available for relationship context.
- The client has been told who owns next steps.

## Sales Close-Out

After onboarding accepts the client:

- Set account stage to `12 Handed To Onboarding`.
- Mark opportunity closed-won.
- Keep sales materials available for future expansion / account management.
- Add any future account-growth opportunities as new opportunities rather than mixing them into onboarding notes.
