Proposed CRM Entity Model

Proposed future-state data model based on the current discussion: separate provisional lead intake from trusted master company data, then manage tenant-specific opportunity and client journeys from that shared foundation.

Master Data Official company identity is global and deduplicated. This is where verification and registrar-sourced data lives.
Tenant Journey Each tenant has its own commercial relationship to the company, with opportunities, activities, materials, and client-state data.
Process Engine One shared process system can target qualification, opportunity, and client flows without duplicating the process definition tree.
